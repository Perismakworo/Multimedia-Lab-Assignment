Vision

To become a leading outcome-driven entrepreneurial research university by translating our excellence into a major contribution to culture, economic well-being, and quality of life.

Mission

To provide all-round quality education in an atmosphere of academic excellence, foster ethical practices, and develop entrepreneurial and leadership skills.

About Strathmore University

Achievements

  • First multiracial university in Kenya.
  • Top-ranked in ICT education in East Africa.
